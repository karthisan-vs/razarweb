Refund & Cancellation Policy

At RapidDrop, we aim for total customer satisfaction. This policy outlines the conditions for cancellations and refunds.

1. Cancellation Conditions

  • Pre-pickup: You can cancel your delivery request at any time before our courier picks up the package for a full refund.
  • Post-pickup: Once a package has been picked up, the service is considered active, and cancellations may not be eligible for a refund.
  • Courier Unavailable: If we are unable to assign a courier to your request within 60 minutes, you may cancel for a full refund.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are applicable if:

  • The service was cancelled by RapidDrop due to operational issues.
  • The customer cancelled the service according to the conditions mentioned above.
  • There was a billing error or duplicate payment.

3. Refund Processing Time

Once a refund is approved, it will be processed and credited back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days. The exact time may vary depending on your bank's policies.
